About Me

My name is Drew Clements and I'm a Graphic Designer & Front-End Web Developer! I started this crazy journey when I found myself in a dead end career and knew I needed to make a change. So I bought beat-up laptop and enrolled in DeVry University's Web & Graphic Design program. And it was the best decision I ever made!

Growing up I've always had a passion for art and I knew at an early age I wanted a career that would let me use that passion. To me, Design is more than just a clever arrangement of colors, shapes, and pretty pictures. Design is the ability to provide Creative & Effective Solutions that seemingly pause time in this fast-paced and ever-evolving world we live in. As a designer its my job to capture the passion you have for your business and build something that allows you to share that passion with the world through brand you're building.

I currently live in middle Georgia with my beautiful wife and our dog Chief! Our world is going to change forever this September when we welcome our baby girl to the world. We're excited to become parents and I can't wait to be a dad!
